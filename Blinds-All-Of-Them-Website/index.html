ABOUT US

Blinds for 35 years,

For 35 years, our business has been a trusted name on the North Coast, KZN. Originally founded as a seller of beds and cane furniture, we have evolved to meet the changing needs of our community. Today, we specialize in providing high-quality blinds, screens, shutters, and awnings. With over 3,000 successful installations, we are proud to be the most experienced provider in the region. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our business since day one. Whether you're looking to enhance your home or business, our expert team is here to help you find the perfect solution tailored to your needs.
